About Me

You can say I'm that Molecular Biology fanatic down the hill with a strange enthusiasm for programming. A strange combo, I know, but I would say my need to understand anything I find complicated trumps normalcy, as a social construct. And oh, I am Hussein, a Molecular Biology major at Pomona College in Claremont, CA. I came here from Ghana in pursuit of a liberal arts education and although it hasn't been easy, the experience has been great.

Interests

When I'm not in the lab or the classroom, I like to try my hands on learning stuff online, especially programming, or to read a good book. "The Martian" by Andy Weir is an all-time favorite and I would recommend codecademy to those interested in learning programming online, and datacamp to those who want to explore topics in data science, statistics, and/ or machine learning. Learning how stuff work never gets boring so feel free to try them out!
